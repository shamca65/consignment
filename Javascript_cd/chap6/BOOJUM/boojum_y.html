Why Boojum?

"For the Snark was a Boojum, you see!"

This started life as Justin Boyan's JavaScript Snark search engine. Now that I've modified it, it is a Boojum.

If you look at the Boojum page with a non-JavaScript capable browser, you will see nothing. The Snark in Lewis Carroll's poem was rather like his Cheshire Cat in that it kept vanishing away. If you want to know more, look at the Snark FAQ.

  • To use the Boojum, you must have a browser that is capable of forms and JavaScript, such as Netscape v2.0 or better.
  • Enter your search terms in the form and select your preferred search engine. You can easily search again with another search engine.
  • You can resize the search panel (to zero size if you want) and so enlarge the display window.
  • You may prefer to use the Snark-to-Go floating window instead
  • The normal Jesus Army Search page has links to some other search engines as well
